## Website Purpose
I'm a Wife is a gentle communication tool that helps married women express their feelings thoughtfully before difficult conversations. It provides a safe space to process emotions, breathe, and craft messages with wisdom instead of anger.

## Key Features
- Emotional processing through guided steps
- Breathing exercises for calming
- Affirmations for validation
- Gift suggestion system for reconciliation
- Email composition assistance
- Auto-send link sharing
- Social media sharing for community support

## Target Audience
- Married women
- Wives seeking healthy communication tools
- Women in committed relationships
- Anyone looking for emotional wellness support

## Use Cases
- Processing hurt feelings before confrontation
- Preventing reactive arguments
- Expressing needs without escalation
- Building healthier relationship communication patterns
- Taking emotional space constructively

## Content Topics
- Relationship communication
- Emotional intelligence
- Marriage wellness
- Conflict resolution
- Self-care in relationships
- Emotional validation
- Mindful communication

## Ethical Guidelines
This tool is designed to:
- Promote healthy communication
- Validate emotional experiences
- Encourage self-reflection
- Support relationship wellness
- Prevent reactive conflict

This tool should NOT be used to:
- Manipulate partners
- Avoid genuine communication
- Replace therapy or professional help
- Enable unhealthy relationship patterns
